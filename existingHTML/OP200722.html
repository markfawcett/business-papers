SUMMER ADJOURNMENT - TABLING OF AMENDMENTS TO PUBLIC BILLS

During the recess, amendments may be submitted by email to pbohoc@parliament.uk from a Member’s network account or from the network account of a member of staff authorised to act on the Member’s behalf.

Deadlines, publication and eligibility for selection

If amendments are to be eligible for selection in proceedings in Committee of the whole House on the Non-Domestic Rating (Public Lavatories) Bill on Thursday 3 September, they should be tabled by 4.30 pm on Friday 28 August. Amendments tabled by this deadline will be available on the relevant bill pages of the Parliamentary website from the evening of Friday 28 August.

It is not expected that any public bill committee will meet in the week beginning Tuesday 1 September.

Summer Adjournment - Applying for a Proxy Vote

On 20 July, the House agreed a motion to extend the proxy voting scheme until 28 September 2020 and to allow Members to apply for a proxy vote during recess.

Members with existing proxy votes

All existing pandemic proxy votes continue in effect until 28 September unless you cancel them. If you want to cancel your proxy and vote in person from 1 September, or if you want to change your nominated proxy, you must email proxyvote@parliament.uk before 4.30pm on Friday 28 August.

Members who want to apply for a new proxy vote

If you want to apply for a proxy vote to come into effect on Tuesday 1 September, you must email proxyvote@parliament.uk before 4.30pm on Friday 28 August, and you must include the name of the Member who is your nominated proxy.

If you have a proxy vote, you must not take part in physical proceedings at Westminster.

Please note that the proxyvote@parliament.uk inbox will not be regularly checked until 26 August, but your request will be processed then.
